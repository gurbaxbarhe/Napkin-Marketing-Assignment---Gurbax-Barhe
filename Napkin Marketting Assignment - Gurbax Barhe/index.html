PROTECTING OUR PEOPLE AND PLACES | REDEFINING RISK

SECURITY AND SAFETY STRATEGIES FOR JEWISH SCHOOLS, SYNAGOGUES AND BUSINESSES

Running a Jewish organization means you’re always on alert. According to Bnat Brith, anti semitic incidents are up 28% since 2012.

If you have responsibility for security of your Jewish organization, synagogue, school or business, this webinar (online seminar) is specifically designed to cover best practices and high-level security strategies to help make your facility safer and smarter when it comes to facing risk.

Arthur J. Gallagher Insurance invites you to gain insights and tools to manage security concerns, better protect your facility and most importantly increase the safety of your members, staff and customers.

“Protecting our People and Places” gives Jewish leaders a strong foundation in approaching security and counter terrorism safety for their organizations and developing a strategic plan for risk management.

  • Conduct a threat and risk assessment to identify weaknesses in your facility, structure

  • Be prepared for minor incidences and worst case scenarios.
  • Plan measure and potential steps prior to special events at your facility.
